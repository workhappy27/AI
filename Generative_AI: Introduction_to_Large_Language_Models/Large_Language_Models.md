#### 🔍What is generarive AI:    

    📚At a high level, we can think of generative AI as an approach to artificial intelligence that is focused on creating content or synthetic data, such as videos, pictures, text, and sound.   

#### 🔍 what is artificial intelligence or AI?    

    📚AI is a branch of computer science that deals with the creation of intelligent agents, which are systems that can reason, learn and act autonomously.
